ENTPs are energized by social interaction, focus more on big ideas than tiny details, make decisions with logic over emotion, and prefer going with the flow instead of sticking to rigid plans. They’re often called “Visionaries” ’cause they’re all about bold, innovative thinking and chasing what’s next.
